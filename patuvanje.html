Paris

My travel experience in Paris was a dream come true. Although I've only been there once, it instantly became my favorite place. Choosing a favorite activity is difficult as I indulged in famous croissants, explored the Louvre, climbed the Eiffel Tower, visited the Opera, and even spent two incredible days at Disneyland. The city's charm and the multitude of attractions made my trip truly spectacular.


Prague

My travel experience in Prague was truly mesmerizing. The captivating architecture and rich culture left a lasting impression. I explored iconic sites such as Wenceslas Square, took a delightful stroll across the Charles Bridge, and marveled at Prague's Astronomical Clock, immersing myself in the city's historical and artistic wonders.


Budapest

My travel experiences in Budapest were unforgettable. Having visited twice, I have countless cherished memories. The stunning Heroes Square, magnificent St. Stephen's Basilica, picturesque City Park, majestic Parliament, and iconic Chain Bridge all left a lasting impression. However, the highlights for me were the breathtaking Buda Castle and the intriguing Dracula's Castle, truly capturing the essence of this enchanting city.


Milan

My travel experience in Milan was a tale of two trips. The first, a school-organized road trip, allowed me only a glimpse of the magnificent Duomo. The second visit granted me ample time to explore Milan's rich heritage, including the exquisite interior of the Duomo, the renowned La Scala opera house, the majestic Sforza Castle, and even a side trip to the picturesque town of Como. Milan's architectural wonders and cultural treasures made both visits truly remarkable.


Venice

My travel experiences in Venice were captivating. The city's unique charm and captivating spots made it an unforgettable destination. From enchanting gondola rides to visiting iconic sites like Piazza di San Marco, Doge's Palace, and the Rialto Bridge, I had the opportunity to explore the beauty and history of this remarkable city. Whether with family or during a high school road trip, Venice left a lasting impression on me.


Amsterdam

My travel experience in Amsterdam was delightful. Visiting with my family, we explored the serene Vondelpark, admired the majestic Royal Palace, and marveled at the iconic Westerkerk. Taking a boat ride along the picturesque canals added to the charm. Additionally, we enjoyed spending two days in Eindhoven, creating wonderful memories in the Netherlands.


Bratislava

My travel experience in Bratislava was truly memorable. During my visit, I stayed in the captivating Old Town, which offered breathtaking views. I explored notable landmarks such as the Blue Church, Bratislava Castle, Old Town Hall, and the National Theater, immersing myself in the city's rich history and architectural beauty.
